Milanesa

Recipe

It is a thin fillet, usually beef, passed through a beaten egg and then breadcrumbs, which is cooked fried or baked and is usually accompanied by a side dish, such as French fries.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1‚ÅÑ4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 lbs boneless beef top sirloin steaks, cut into 1/4 inch slices.
  • oil

Steps

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs and milk.
  2. In another shallow bowl, combine the bread crumbs and salt.
  3. Dip steak in egg mixture, then coat with the crumb mixture.
  4. In a large skillet, heat 1/2" of oil.
  5. Brown steak over medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes on each side or until meat reaches desired doneness.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
